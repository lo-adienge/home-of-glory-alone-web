ABOUT US

HOGA is a community based organization located in Kisumu County, Seme Sub-County, ... Ward.
HOGA operates in .... but has interests and activities through enterprises and community support programs within and outside the sub-County.
The CBO draws its 60-plus membership from within and beyond Kisumu County of Kenya.
The organization operates several products aimed at achieving the five pillars targeting the members, the public, and the global community.
HOGA seeks to partner with like-minded organizations to deliver the products and services and facilitate going to scale with its unique model and lessons.

Our Vision

A vibrant organization with stable, self-reliant and empowered members able to participate, and make informed decisions for socioeconomic transformation.

Our Mission

To foster holistic transformation of members, their families and society through innovative and practical action for quality economic, social and environmental status.

Our Values

In the pursuit of its goals and strategic objectives, we upholds to the values which we promise to society, our partners and members.
